About Me



I became intrigued with Philosophy of Technology while researching and writing my Political Science senior thesis on the role of the internet, and the influence of hip-hop as a form of democratic political dissent, in the youth-led "Arab Spring" uprisings of 2011. Once my interest in the social and political effects of technology met with my curiosity about quantum mechanic's Holographic Universe Theory, my casual desire to "learn how to code someday" piqued. As a humanities graduate and an aspiring web developer in my spare time, I enjoy reading and writing about the interconnectedness of humans and the technology we invent that changes us.
